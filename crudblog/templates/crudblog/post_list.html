So all this started from that phone call, in which Karan told Harshita that he liked her.
That call changed everything, friendships, bonds and eventually paved the way for high end cut-class bakchodi.
The next day, when Karan arrived school, he felt a totally different atmosphere. He could not understand
why everyone was staring at him. Some even congratulated him. He was very puzzeled. His brain was asking just one
thing, "WHAT THE HELL IS GOING ON?". He started thinkig of any possible clue of this atmosphere in the past, but got none.
In between querries and congratulations, he spotted Aditya. As expected, he spilled his very first statement,
"bhai, apne bhai k sath aisa krega? Bina koi khabar bandi pata liya? Party kab dega?"
"Seriously??? was this that big issue?", Karan asked himself. He excused himself from Aditya
saying, "treat will be soon".


As Karan entered the class, the first person he saw was her. He asked Harshita, "we were supposed to disclose this together".
Harshita nodded and with an innocent smile said that she only told Shikha about this, but it just got viral.
That day was passed gossiping on this topic only. The main dabate was about to come when the three friends were going to meet.
